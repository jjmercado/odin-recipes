Favorite dish of yagara Bulls: Steamed water meat

Ingredients

  • 500g pork spare ribs, 1/2 tsp salt
  • A little black pepper, 2 stalks of celery
  • 1/2 carrot (100g), 1/4 radish
  • 1/2 lettuce

    • Sauce

  • 1/2 spring onion, 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 100ml ponzu, garlic to taste

Description

  1. Place spareribs, salt and black pepper in a plastic bag and knead well, then let rest for about an hour. Using a peeler, thinly peel the celery, carrot and radish. Tear the lettuce leaves into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Roughly chop the spring onion for the sauce and place in an ovenproof bowl. Heat the sesame oil in a pan, pour over the spring onion, add the ponzu and stir.
  3. Steam the spare ribs in the preheated steamer for 20 minutes. When the meat is tender, add vegetables and steam until cooked. Then serve together with the sauce. Add grated garlic to taste.
