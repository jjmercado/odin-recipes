Ruffy's favorite: Bone Meat

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken legs
  • 4 eggs
  • 30g breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • a
    • 500g chicken mince
    • 1 tsp salt, 1 egg, some black pepper
  • Salad oil to taste

Directions

  1. Form tulips from the chicken thighs. With kitchen scissors from the direction that will later be the handle, separate the meat and tendons from the bone as if you were scraping it. When you have separated it to the end of the bone, turn the meat upside down.
  2. Soak the breadcrumbs in the milk. Put in a bowl, add the kneaded ingredients [a] and knead again.
  3. Wrap the cooked eggs with the meat of the chicken thighs. If it does not adhere well, lightly score the meat. Rub hands thinly with salad oil and surround the chicken thighs and eggs with the mixture from (2).
  4. grill in oven preheated to 200Â°C for 15-20 minutes under observation.
